April 8, 2024

There will be a total solar eclipse on April 8th, 2024, along the red line of totality on the map above. Even locations that are over 1,000 miles away from either side of this line may observe a partial solar eclipse.

The total eclipse will begin at sunrise, 6:40 AM CKT (UTC-10) in the South Pacific Ocean near Kiribati and the Cook Islands, and end at 6:55 PM AZOT (UTC-1) near sunset in the North Atlantic.

The point of maximum totality will begin at 12:17 PM CST (UTC-6) near Rancho Orlanda in Durango, Mexico.
